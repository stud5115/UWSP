Who are we?

Upward Spiral Fuels is an BEE level 1 compliant, established company with an integrated
focus in the trading of petroleum products.We supply petroleum products as a wholesaler to larger
commercial clients. Our main clientele includes depots, retailers, mining, agricultural, white site
filling stations and similar commercial clients whereby we supply their wholesale petroleum product needs,
we now have a retail filling station for your convenience should you require smaller fillups .



About our services

Delivery Upward Spiral Fuels owns and controls the intermediate supply chain between manufacturer and client
along the dimensions of supply, storage and transport. Supply Upward Spiral Fuels extracts directly from the major
fuel suppliers of Sasol, Engen, Total and therefore we can assure our clients of the highest quality fuel.
Storage Our storage depot can hold in excess of 300 000lts of fuel. 50ppm diesel, ULP and illuminating paraffin.
Transport All transport is carried out by vehicles owned and managed by Upward Spiral Fuels and this allows us to
optimise the most efficient and effective delivery schedule speed and service is key with us. Ship to Trade We cater
for the intermediate bulk loads of 2000L to 10000L through our express division. Value added Service We offer
drivers resting facilities, clean toilets and showers. We also assist with vehicle breakdowns, repairs to our clients.
Retail Division Upward Spiral Fuels has ventured into the retail market with a new service station in anticipation of
reaching out to our smaller clients, a step in becomming more effecient and more convenient to clients,
our apporoach to service in a new form of thinking. Why Us? 24hr service delivery Industrial experience Competitive pricing
After sales support Express divisions that never leaves your business at the mercy of delayed product or product issues.
Additional Services Storage Dispencing Equipment and Maintenance Pump installations Tank, Pipe works as well as electrical work.
