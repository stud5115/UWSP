About us

Upward Spiral Fuels is an BEE level 1 compliant, established company with an integrated focus in the trading of petroleum products.We supply petroleum products as a wholesaler to
larger commercial Clients. Our main clientele includes depots, retailers, mining, agricultural,
white site filling stations and similar commercial clients whereby we supply their wholesale
petroleum product needs, we now have a retail filling station for your convenience should you require smaller fillups .
